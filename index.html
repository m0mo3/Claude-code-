Questions

Everything you
need to know.

How much is entry and what does the ticket include?
The entry for the event is CHF 14.90, this will include raffle tickets for a chance to win special prizes during the event.
Who are Nicolas Chambers and Seric Critchley?
Nico & Seric are Swiss national rowers and you can learn more about them on the about us page.
Where exactly does the money go?
The money will fund Nico & Seric's journey to LA 2028. It will go to things such as training, equipment, flight tickets, and more to support them.
Can I donate without attending?
Yes, you can donate without attending. The donation will be available at the top of the page.
How do I get to HIM campus, and is there parking?
HIM campus is located at Avenue des Alpes 15, Vaud, 1820, Montreux. It is a 5 minute walk from the train station and is situated beautifully over Lac Léman.
Will food and drinks be available, and are they included?
Finger food and drinks will be available to all guests who attend the event.
