Where it goes

Your donation funds the journey.

The gap between national champion and Olympic athlete is not closed by talent alone. It takes training camps, racing fees, equipment, international travel, recovery, and the hundreds of costs that accumulate over a four-year Olympic cycle. Every franc donated through Alps2LA goes directly toward those costs for Nico Chambers and Seric Critchley.

There is no overhead, no organisation in the middle. This is a student-led fundraiser run by friends. The money goes to the athletes, and the athletes go to Los Angeles.
